Our Story

Here at Charlie’s Cafe we understand not only the importance of quality beverages, but also the importance of community. We strive to create a positive experience for all of our customers by creating genuine connections with our guests, timely service, and providing a community space for all of our customers to collaborate and create. Charlie’s is more than coffee, we are a community.
